About me

Hi,

My name is Nicolas Deville. You can call me Nic.

I'm French-Austrian, grew up in Paris, living now in Germany after 10 years in London.

I have been in Sales most of my life, starting when I was a kid, working with my mother for pocket money, helping her as a freelance B2B rep in the 90's!

I have climbed the Sales career ladder to be the youngest VP Sales, then Managing Director Europe (2x) - both times in VC-backed SaaS startups, encompassing leadership of all local functions (Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, Operations) with a team of 30+.

Both companies IPO'ed.

Left the "employee" world in 2017. Bootstrapped and sold two platforms (Convolus.com and OfficeBots.io) - both to US organisations.

In 2020, the pandemic meant an opportunity to go back to the world of virtual events, and the opportunity to start offering my skills and experience, providing "Sales-as-a-Service" to US B2B SaaS looking to expand in Europe - doing what I do best, while providing me with a better life balance.

I love the Game of Sales 😁
